How to stay motivated while learing to code?

You have decided to learn to code! You feel excited and super motivated after quick yutube tutorials. Feeling optimistic and eager to spend every single free minute to devote to learning. After a few days or weeks you get stuck with a problem you can not resolve. But in reality, there is always a solution and a way out! But... You get discouraged. You bitterly come to the realization that you will not be anywhere near to get ready to apply for a new job in the nearest future. And the definition of the "nearest future" is becoming too indefinite... Finally, you decide to take a break... Not to get in this trap, try the following:

Make a plan

  • Break the goal into smaller milestones, completing small projects instead of one big project.
  • Make realistic goals, which are achievable at the current level of knowledge.
  • Set a final destination. Get an answer to what do you want to achieve and which big projetc to make?

Keep being motivated

  • Set your own pace and choose quality over speed.
  • Find a community of same minded people, share your achievments and work on projects together.
  • Avoid burnout and get enough rest. The work & rest balance is super important to stay motivated.

Practice

  • Do not forget that 30% of theory and 70% of coding will bring the best results! So keep creating projects!
